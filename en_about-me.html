About me

My name is Michaela Clark

I’m curious by nature and my curiosity leads me to explore and connect different, at first glance unrelated, directions. Mainly visual arts and movement.

I fell in love with movement thanks to aikido. Japanese martial arts that’s a bit different from all the other ones I’ve met so far. To be in touch, to find a common direction and a path that leads to harmony. To be aware of my own center, axis and breath.

Only then I can be in touch with the world and with myself at the same time. And from that position shiatsu can happen. A touch that gives space for relaxation. A moment when you can feel yourself and find comfort in your body.

But the same set up works for painting or the way of tea as well.

I’m looking for a way, how to live. At the moment I can be found making tea in Jedna Báseň, blues dancing at socials from Blues Dose or studying shibari from Diana Vera.

Thank you all that I had the honor to meet. You keep showing me different sides of life and teaching me…

I’m also looking forward to meeting those of you that I’ve not had the chance to meet yet. To all the shared (extra)ordinary moments.
